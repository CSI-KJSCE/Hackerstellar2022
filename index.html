What is HackerStellar?

Computer Society of India - a students' chapter of the K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering proudly introduces HackerStellar, as a part of our annual flagship council tech event called TechNext. For an incredible 24 hours, you will work together with your teams to envision and develop the technology of tomorrow for solving the problems of today. It is a plethora of opportunities for tech savvies to boost their skills, capitalize on tech trends, both new and old, and get their talent acquainted and appreciated by experts from the industry. Frequently Asked Questions


What is a ‚Äúhackathon‚Äù?

+

A hackathon is usually a day long (can be 36 hours, 48 hours, etc.) coding competition where software programmers, developers, designers, etc. come together to build and design something cool.

What do people normally make?

+

Hacks generally fall under one (or more) of the six cortices: Web Development, App Development, AI/ML, Education, Health, and Financial Technology. Depending upon the problem statements, participants use any of the above.

What are the topics and prizes of hackathon?

+

The topic and the prizes will be declared soon. So keep an eye on the CSI-KJSCE Facebook Page. Also once you've registered with an e-mail id, we'll shoot you a mail about further announcements.

Who can attend HackerStellar?

+

Anyone with the enthusiasm for coding can attend this hackathon. The hackathon is for all age groups and for all the coders.

How much does it cost to attend HackerStellar?

+

Hackerstellar is free for all participants, thanks to our amazing sponsors!

When and where is the event going to be held?

+

HackerStellar is going to be completely virtual! Schedule and details of the event will be updated here soon.

What if I've never been to a hackathon before?

+

Not to worry, We welcome students of all skill levels. Hackathons can be a great place to learn new skills in a short amount of time. Just be eager to learn, and excited to meet lots of awesome people.

How do teams work?

+

You can work on a project on your own, or you can work with a team. Team size must not exceed four members.

How many members in a team?

+

A team consists of 2-4 members.
